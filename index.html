About

A.Z. Riel is a four-time award winning Teacher of the Year who is inspirational and spirited. He loves archetypes, satire, literature, chips, science, technology, engineering, art, and math. His debut was in sixth grade as a comic artist for the school paper, and his first major work produced was a one-act play as a high school Senior.

He has won a couple writing competitions for his prose and poetry. Riel has a Master’s in Education and fifteen years of teaching experience to support his theories. A.Z. Riel is changing the narrative by talking UP to children.
